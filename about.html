Who Is BLACKPINK?

Blackpink (Hangul: 블랙핑크; commonly stylized as BLACKPINK or BLΛƆKPIИK) is a South Korean girl group formed by YG Entertainment, consisting of members Jisoo, Jennie, Rosé, and Lisa. They debuted on August 8, 2016 with their single album Square One, which spawned "Whistle" and "Boombayah", their first number-one hits in South Korea and on the Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart, respectively.

Blackpink is the highest-charting female Korean act on the Billboard Hot 100, peaking at number 33 with their 2020 single "How You Like That" and collaboration with Lady Gaga "Sour Candy". They also peaked at number 22 on the Billboard 200 with their extended play "Kill This Love." They were the first Korean girl group to enter and top Billboard's Emerging Artists chart and to have three number-one singles on Billboard's World Digital Song Sales chart.

Blackpink became the first female Korean act to receive a certification from the Recording Industry Association of America for their 2018 hit single "Ddu-Du Ddu-Du", which is the most-viewed Korean group music video on YouTube at 1.2B views. The group has the most top 40 hits in the United Kingdom among all Korean artists, with "Kiss and Make Up" (in collaboration with Dua Lipa) being the first-ever song by a Korean group to receive a certification from the British Phonographic Industry. The single was also the first-ever song by a Korean group to be certified platinum by the Australian Recording Industry Association.

The group has broken numerous online records throughout their career; "Kill This Love" (2019) and "How You Like That" each set a record for the most-viewed music video within the first 24 hours of release, with the latter setting four other Guinness World Records and becoming the fastest Korean track to reach double platinum on QQ Music, China's biggest music streaming platform.

Blackpink's accolades also include the New Artist of the Year Awards at the 31st Golden Disc Awards and the 26th Seoul Music Awards and recognition as the most powerful celebrities in South Korea by Forbes Korea in 2019. The group was also the first female Korean group to be included in Forbes' 30 Under 30 Asia list.

They have performed on Coachella and have held concerts all over the world during their World Tour. Blackpink is currently the most-followed female group on Spotify and the most-subscribed music group on YouTube with 41.7m subscribers.

In addition to their musical accomplishments, the group has found success in various other facets as well. All members have been and are currently endorsed by brands such as Chanel, Yves Saint Laurent, Celine, Dior, Samsung, Adidas, Sprite, Moonshot, and other establishments such as banks and cosmetic companies. Wielding massive influence individually and as a group, they quickly sell out products they endorse and advertise. All four members are the most followed K-pop idols on Instagram and have a massive following on other platforms such as Weibo, Vlive, and Tik Tok.

At present, the group is confirmed to release their first full-length album in September 2020. Following the release of the album, members Rosé, Lisa, and Jisoo will release their individual projects, with Rosé’s solo album releasing first. In the midst of comeback preparations, YG Entertainment launched Blackpink's newest reality show, "24/365 with Blackpink", which can be viewed through YouTube. The show documents their comeback alongside sharing their daily lives through vlogs.

Meet The Members

Jisoo

Birth Name: Kim Jisoo (김지수)
Birthday: January 3, 1995
Ethnicity: Korean
Place of Birth: Gunpo, South Korea
Band Role: Lead Vocalist & Visual
Instagram: @sooyaaa__

 Jisoo joined YG Entertainment through auditions as a trainee in 2011 and trained for 5 years. As the oldest member of BLACKPINK, she frequently steps up to the plate as the unofficial leader, often speaking for the group during TV and award show appearances. Jisoo speaks fluent Korean, Japanese, and basic Mandarin. She does not speak English often, but her bandmates have said she understands it quite well. Besides her success as a member of BLACKPINK, Jisoo has shown her talents in other roles as well. In 2017, Jisoo was a MC (host) for popular music show Inkigayo. She has also made a cameo appearance on KBS' drama "The Producers", starred in multiple music videos from other artists, and in many commercial films (by herself and the rest of BLACKPINK). Currently in 2020, she is a brand ambassador for the luxury brands Burberry and Dior. Many consider Jisoo as one of the contenders for Korea's beauty standard. At the moment, she has over 25m followers on Instagram where she uploads pictures/videos of her, her bandmates, and her dog Dalgom.

Jennie

Birth Name: Kim Jennie (김제니)
Birthday: January 16, 1996
Ethnicity: Korean
Place of Birth: Seoul, South Korea
Band Role: Main Rapper & Lead Vocalist
Instagram: @jennierubyjane | @lesyeuxdenini

 During her childhood, Jennie moved and lived in Auckland, New Zealand for 5 years, during which she learned to speak fluent English, Korean, and basic Japanese. She returned to Seoul in 2010 and became a trainee under YG Entertainment, where she trained for just shy of 6 years. While training under the company, she made apperances on songs by labelmates Big Bang's G-Dragon and Lee Hi. As the group's main rapper, Jennie gives off a fierce and charismatic appearance while performing on stage. In November 2018, Jennie released her first single "SOLO" which claimed the number one spot on Gaon Music Chart (Korea's Billboard) for two consecutive weeks. The music video for SOLO currently has over 490 million views on YouTube. Jennie is endorsed by luxury brand Chanel and has appeared on numerous commercial films (by herself and the rest of BLACKPINK). She was also a regular member of a Korean variety TV show named "Village Survival, the Eight." She has two dogs, Kuma and Kai, who sometimes makes appearances on her Instagram which has over 29.5m followers.

Rosé

Birth Name (Korean): Park Chae Young (박채영)
Birth Name (English): Roseanne Park
Birthday: February 11, 1997
Ethnicity: Korean
Place of Birth: Auckland, New Zealand
Band Role: Main Vocalist & Lead Dancer
Instagram: @roses_are_rosie

 Roseanne, better known as Rosé, spent most of her early life in Melbourne, Australia. She had a passion for singing even as a child and was performed with her church's choir. In 2012, Rosé's father discovered an audition YG Entertainment was holding in Sydney, Australia. Rosé partook in the audition and came in first, beating out 700 other auditions and became a trainee under YG Entertainment the same day, training for 4 years. As Rosé is fluent in English, Korean, and Japanese, she often takes over the role of speaking for the group during English presentations. As the group's main vocalist Rosé's distinct vocal timbre is largely met with praise. Other artists and critics note that her unique voice can be easily recognized and "is a type of voice young people love." She is also proficient at playing the piano and guitar and can be seen doing so on music shows and livestreams. Just like with all other BLACKPINK memebers, Rosé has appeared on many commercial films solo and with the group. In 2020, she is officially the luxury brand Yves Saint Laurent's global ambassador. Her solo debut has been announced but has not been released yet. Rosé has a following of more than 26.5m followers on her Instagram where she occasionally interacts with her fans live.

Lisa

Birth Name: Pranpriya Manoban (ปราณปริยา มโนบาล)
Birth Name (Legally changed): Lalisa Manoban (ลลิสา มโนบาล)
Birthday: March 27, 1997
Ethnicity: Thai
Place of Birth: Bangkok, Thailand
Band Role: Main Dancer & Lead Rapper
Instagram: @lalalalisa_m
YouTube: Lilifilm Official

 BLACKPINK's youngest member was born in Thailand where she learned to dance and sing at a very young age. In 2010, Lisa competed in the YG Entertainment Competition where she placed first and was offered a spot to become a YG Entertainment trainee. Though not speaking the language, Lisa moved to Seoul in 2011 to begin her training which lasted 5 years. At present, Lisa can fluently speak English, Korean, Thai, Japanese, and basic Mandarin. Often cited as one of the best dancers in the industry, Lisa brings immense stage presence during her performances. She was invited to be a dance mentor on the Chinese show "Youth With You" which is rare for foreigners. Fan videos of her dancing has garnered millions of views on YouTube. On her own YouTube channel, which has 3.6m subscribers, she posts vacation montage vlogs and dance performances which have tens of millions of views. Lisa is the muse for the luxury brand Celine and has also starred in numerous commercial films in Korea and Thailand. She can be found frequently posting content of her cats Leo, Luca, Lily, and Louis on her Instagram which boasts a 36m followers following.
